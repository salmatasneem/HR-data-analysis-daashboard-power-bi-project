🚀 **Power BI Project – HR Data Analysis Dashboard**

I recently completed a **Power BI dashboard project focused on HR analytics** for a company scenario (*Vignesh InfoTech*). The goal of this project was to transform raw HR data into meaningful insights that help organizations better understand their workforce and support data-driven decision making.

📊 **Key Features of the Dashboard**

🔹 **HR Summary Page**

* Presents key workforce metrics including **total headcount and gender distribution**.
* Provides a quick overview of organizational workforce statistics.

🔹 **Headcount Analysis**

* **Year-on-Year headcount trends** to analyze workforce growth.
* **Headcount breakdown by gender** to evaluate diversity.
* **Age group distribution** to understand workforce demographics.
* **Department-wise headcount analysis** to identify workforce allocation.
* **Country-wise headcount insights** for geographical workforce distribution.

🔹 **Interactive Analysis with Slicers**

* Dynamic filtering using **Department, Country, and Employee Name** slicers.
* Enables users to explore insights interactively and drill down into specific segments.

🔹 **Advanced Visualizations**

* Department & Gender comparison charts
* Age group distribution analysis
* Headcount trends across months
* Hierarchical breakdown by Country → City → Business Unit → Department → Job Title

🔹 **Data Refresh Indicator**

* Displays **last data refresh date and time**, ensuring users always know how current the data is.

💡 **Impact of the Dashboard**
This dashboard helps organizations gain a **clear understanding of workforce structure, diversity, and distribution**, enabling better HR planning and strategic decision making.

🛠 **Tools Used:** Power BI | Data Modeling | DAX | Interactive Visualizations
